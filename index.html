About The Event

About The Event

Youth Employability Summit - YES2021 is a two-day capacity building initiative organized under the office of the National Vice president to be hosted across the six geo-political zones of Nigeria starting with North Central. This event will feature keynote sessions, soft-skill training sessions to be facilitated by Jobberman Nigeria, panel discussions, and competitions. The summit will bring together students, policymakers, IT professionals, Start-up founders and Experts in the tech space within and outside Nigeria to share, guide and mentor youth towards supporting the current workforce and creating start-ups that contribute meaningfully to the nation and continents economy.
